👋 Welcome

Welcome to ITI Studies — a free MCQ practice platform for ITI students across Gujarat, India. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we use your data.

📋 Information We Collect

When you use ITI Studies, we may collect your name and email address if you choose to login with Google, your quiz scores and progress data to track your improvement, and general usage information to improve our service.

We do NOT collect sensitive personal information, financial data, or any information beyond what is needed to provide our service.

🎯 How We Use Your Information

We use your information to save your quiz progress across devices, show your performance history and results, improve our question bank and app features, and provide a personalized learning experience.

📢 Google AdSense & Cookies

ITI Studies uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google AdSense may use cookies to show relevant ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

🔐 Third Party Services

We use Firebase by Google for authentication and data storage. Your data is stored securely on Google's servers as per their privacy standards. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for complete details.

👶 Children's Privacy

ITI Studies is designed for students aged 14 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

🛡️ Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.
